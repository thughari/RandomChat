Community Guidelines

Welcome! To keep our community safe and fun for everyone, please agree to these simple rules:

  • Be Respectful: Harassment, hate speech, racism, sexism, or any abusive behavior is strictly forbidden.
  • No Inappropriate Content: Do not stream nudity, sexually explicit material, or anything else that is not safe for a general audience.
  • No Illegal Activities: Do not promote, engage in, or display any illegal acts.
